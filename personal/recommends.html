Recommendations

Here is unordered list of blogs, articles and pages in general that I found worth reading. It does not mean that I approve or agree everything posted by these authors, just that I found it interesting.

  • Blog of Ali Grudi, author of fbpdf. 1
  • Blog of Chris Wellons, author of enchive, with interesting musing on emacs and gpg. 2
  • Blog of Natasha Layla Marchant, with convincing critics of FSF work3 and good article on why javascript my die4.
  • Series of articles, evaluating different, free and proprietary, software to what degree it is spyware.5
  • Nice article about behaviour of different file systems on crashes6
  • Brilliant article “Web Obesity”7
  • Your Big data is not big, by Adam Drake8
  • Bash programming wiki.9 Not that you should program in bash, but sometimes you have (e.g. Nix, Gentoo).
  • Weather forecast with curl10. It is awesome.
  • A curated list of awesome command-line frameworks, toolkits, guides and gizmos11. It has much better signal/noise rate than random web search.
  • Diary of man, looking to change his habits to improve his productivity.12 Interesting articles, terrible web-design. Open in lynx(1).
